Omurice Recipe

An irresistible combination of fried rice wrapped in a silky omelet, topped with ketchup. A Japanese favorite!

Preparation Time

  • Total: 30 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup diced chicken
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1 tbsp ketchup (plus more for garnish)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Sauté the fillings: Heat oil in a pan, sauté diced chicken and onions until golden brown.
  2. Add rice and seasoning: Add cooked rice, ketchup, and soy sauce. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. Set aside.
  3. Cook the omelet: Beat eggs with a pinch of salt. Heat a non-stick pan and pour in the eggs, tilting the pan to cover the surface evenly.
  4. Wrap the rice: Place the fried rice on one half of the omelet, then carefully fold the other half over the rice.
  5. Serve: Slide the Omurice onto a plate. Garnish with ketchup and serve warm.
